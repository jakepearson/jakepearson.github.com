###Ingredients
* 1 granny smith apple (cored) 
* 1 orange (peeled)
* ~8 pineapple chunks
* 2 tsp chia seeds
* 2 leaves of kale
* 1-2 handfuls of spinach
* 2 cups of water/ice (kinda guessing)

###Optional
* Quarter sized chunk of ginger
* 1/4 lemon (peeled)
* Small handful of parsley
