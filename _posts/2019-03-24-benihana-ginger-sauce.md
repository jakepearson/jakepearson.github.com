# Ingredients

* 5 ounces of sliced onion, rinsed in running water for 15 minutes
* 2 ounces ginger, peeled and sliced
* 1 lemon, peeled and chopped, squeezed
* 2 cups soy sauce
* 1 cup vinegar

# Instructions

1. Combine ingredients in blender
2. Keeps for 1 week
